Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

SongDrop ("SongDrop", "we", "us") is operated by [COMPANY], [ADDRESS], Estonia. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and your rights. For any privacy question, contact us at [EMAIL].

1. Who this applies to

  • DJs: people who create a SongDrop account.
  • Guests: people who scan a DJ's QR code and request songs. Guests do not create an account.

2. Data we collect

DJs (account holders):

  • Account & identity: email address, and name if provided, via our authentication provider (Clerk). If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address.
  • Profile: your chosen page slug, display name, tagline, accent color, and any avatar/backdrop image you upload.
  • YouTube connection: when you connect YouTube, we store an encrypted Google OAuth refresh token, your selected playlist, and connection status. We request these scopes:
    • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube — to read your playlists and add requested videos to the playlist you choose.
    • email / userinfo.email — to confirm the connected Google account.
  • Billing: subscription status and Stripe customer/subscription identifiers. Card details are handled by Stripe and never stored by us.

Guests:

  • Song requests you submit (track title, artist, video reference, timestamp, status) and an optional name you may type. No account, no contact details are required.

Automatically:

  • Basic technical/log data (e.g. IP address, device/browser type) processed by our hosting providers for security and reliability.

3. Google / YouTube API data

SongDrop uses YouTube API Services and the Google APIs. By connecting YouTube you also agree to the YouTube Terms of Service and acknowledge the Google Privacy Policy.

  • We access your YouTube playlists and add videos you approve to the playlist you select. We do not read, post, or modify anything else.
  • SongDrop's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
  • We use Google/YouTube data only to provide the SongDrop features described here. We do not use it for advertising, do not sell it, and do not allow humans to read it except as required for security, to comply with law, or with your consent.
  • You can revoke SongDrop's access at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions. Revoking access stops further playlist updates; we delete the stored connection on your request or on disconnection.

4. How we use data

  • Provide the service: run your page, process guest requests, add approved tracks to your playlist, show your live feed, and bill your subscription.
  • Secure and improve the service and prevent abuse.

We do not sell personal data and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

5. Subprocessors / third parties

Clerk (authentication) · Stripe (payments) · Google / YouTube (playlist access) · Cloudflare R2 (image storage) · Neon (database, EU region) · Vercel (web hosting) · Render (API hosting). Each processes data only to provide their function.

6. Storage & retention

Data is stored in the EU (database in the EU region). OAuth refresh tokens are encrypted at rest. We keep account and request data while your account is active and delete it within a reasonable period after account closure or on valid request, except where we must retain records by law.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

You may request access, correction, deletion, export, or restriction of your personal data, and you may object to processing. Contact [EMAIL]. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate (AKI).

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for sign-in/session and payments (set by Clerk and Stripe). We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Children

SongDrop is not directed to children under 16 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; we will post the new "Last updated" date here and, for material changes, notify account holders.
